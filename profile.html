Kyle Ruane

Howdy

I’m a multidisciplinary designer from Pittsburgh (by way of the Poconos) specializing in interfaces and branding. By proxy, I'm also an accidental front-end dev, occasional brand strategist, and illustrator when needed. I like to consider myself a generalist, frequently

 

Currently

Ofiicially a senior product designer, but really more of a utility player at everyone's favorite t-shirt company Cotton Bureau, where I pitch in on design, front-end, coffee brewing, and obscure movie references. I even put an occasional shirt up for sale.

 

Previously

Co-founded a startup called Plain along with @cdevroe. We built a couple of online products—an inline website editing system called Barley and a to-do list for bookmarks called Unmark.

Ran an agency with @timwco called Argyll Studios. We specialized in custom WordPress sites and made an online password management tool called Hosted Notes.

A freelance designer, performing contract-based work for a number of mid-sized and large agencies, on both short and long-term engagements.

Design lead at a mid-sized eCommerce agency called Solid Cactus. I worked on both client projects and internal products, designing over 100 web stores for nearly every type of business—from small mom-and-pop stores to Fortune 100 corporations.

A swiss-army-knife at a small local advertising agency where I managed projects, designed for print/web/TV, developed an occasional website, handled media buying, and copy-wrote.

Art director for a local large-format lifestyle magazine, handling editorial graphics, ad creation, layout, and print preparation/proofing.
